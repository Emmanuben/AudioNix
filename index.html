🎵 AudioNix – Playlist to MP3 Made Simple

AudioNix is a free, open-source desktop app that lets you download music from your Spotify playlists without needing Spotify Premium or developer credentials.

✨ Features

  • 🎧 Paste any public Spotify playlist link
  • 📥 AudioNix finds the matching tracks on YouTube
  • ⚡ One-click downloads in MP3 format
  • 🖥️ No logins, no setup, no hassle

🚀 How It Works

AudioNix uses open-source tools like SpotDL and yt-dlp to download songs that match your Spotify playlists from YouTube. No Spotify streaming is involved.
